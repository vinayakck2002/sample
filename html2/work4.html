Delicious Choclate Chip Cookies

preparation time:30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl,cream together butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs,one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. in a separate bowl,combine the flour, baking soda; gradually add to the butter mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
